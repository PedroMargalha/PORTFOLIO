ABOUT ME

I born in Beja, in 1986.

Since young I realized that my life will be related with engineering. I grow up surrounded by projects and new buildings where some of my relatives worked.

Due to that, and because in that time I thought that it was my vocation, I went to Lisbon in the age of 18.In Instituto Superior Técnico I studied Civil Engineering and I did a post graduation in Property Valuation.

After that I came back to Beja, my hometown. In 2013, I decided to enroll in the bachelor degree of Computer Science.
