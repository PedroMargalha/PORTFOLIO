ABOUT ME

I was born in Beja, in 1986.

Since my youth I realized that my life will be related with engineering. I grew up surrounded by projects and new buildings where some of my relatives worked.

Due to that I thought that it is my vocation. At the age of 18 I decided to go to Lisbon where I studied Civil Engineering in Instituto Superior Técnico and Real Estate Property Valuation at FUNDEC.

After that I came back to my hometown Beja. In 2013 I decided to enroll in the bachelor degree of Computer Science.

October 2015 - Until this moment

Full-stack Web Development



Online specialization of Coursera Platform, developed by Hong Kong Science and Technology University.

This specialization is a group of five courses and a final project. It includes client-side development using HTML/CSS, JavaScript/JQuery, and frameworks like AngularJs and Bootstrap.

In the server side, it is developed implementations of NoSQL Databases using MongoDB, inside a Node.Js environment and RESTul API to communicate with the client-side. Furthermore, in this specialization are used the frameworks IONIC and CORDOVA for building a hybrid Mobil application.

In the final project is applied all the knowledge for creating a totally functional web and hybrid Mobil application with back-end support.

BME4:"Danube Bridges in Budapest" - Athens Program.

Course about the role and importance of the steel bridges.

The Budapest bridges are a great opportunity for everyone. For build bridges is necessary a great variety of engineering knowledges, since the foundation and superstructure until the traffic planning of the bridge.

In this course were presented bridges of steel and iron, and it was summarized the problems in design, detailing, construction, maintaining and rehabilitation of bridges.

It was developed a detailed treatment of aspects of both traditional and modern bridges, as modern bridges are to be built and traditional bridges are to be repaired or reconstructed.
